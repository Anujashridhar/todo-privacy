Privacy Policy for NextTask Todo

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NextTask Todo ("the Extension," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you install and use the Extension. Our core philosophy is strict data privacy through zero-knowledge architecture. We cannot read your tasks, and we do not collect your personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

NextTask Todo is designed to operate entirely locally and through your existing Google account infrastructure.

  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, trackers, or telemetry to monitor how you use the Extension.
  • No Developer Access: The developer of this Extension has zero access to your tasks, your browsing history, your master password, or any other personal data.
  • No External Servers: Your data is never transmitted to any external databases or third-party servers operated by us.

2. How Your Data is Stored and Synced

The Extension requires the storage permission to save your tasks and preferences.

  • Local and Sync Storage: Your data is saved locally on your device and synchronized across your other devices using Chrome’s native sync API.
  • Google Account Integration: The synchronization process is handled entirely by Google, utilizing the Google account you are logged into within your Chrome browser.

3. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

To ensure your data remains completely private, NextTask Todo employs robust cryptographic security:

  • Client-Side Encryption: All task data is heavily encrypted directly on your device using the Web Crypto API (AES-GCM) before it is saved to your local disk or transmitted to Google's sync servers.
  • Master Password Security: Your encryption key is derived from a Master Password using PBKDF2. Your Master Password is never saved, transmitted, or logged. It exists entirely in your memory and temporarily in your browser's active session RAM. If you lose your Master Password, your data cannot be recovered.

4. Permissions Required

The Extension requests the absolute minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • storage: Required to save your encrypted vault locally and sync it across your devices.
  • sidePanel: Required to display the Todo list interface persistently alongside your active browser tabs.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You maintain complete control over your data.

  • In-App Deletion: You can permanently delete individual tasks directly within the Extension interface.
  • Complete Erasure: You can completely wipe the encrypted vault from your local machine and your Google Sync account by uninstalling the Extension and clearing your Chrome sync data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technical architecture or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Effective Date."

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the security practices of NextTask Todo, please contact the developer via the support link provided in the Chrome Web Store listing.
